Who We Are

Rooted in Rwanda. Committed to Wellness.

Isoko Life Center Ltd is a wellness and beauty company based in Kigali, Rwanda. We source, curate, and supply premium natural supplements, herbal remedies, and beauty products for individuals and families seeking a healthier lifestyle.

Our name — Isoko — means "source" in Kinyarwanda. We are a source of trusted, quality products and expert wellness guidance for our community.
